History

Denmark is the world's oldest kingdom. Whereas, Copenhagen is Scandinavia's largest city and its history goes way back. There are evidences that Copenhagen existed as a settlement more than 6,000 years ago. Among cobbled squares, narrow streets and old buildings the history lives with us to this day, which attract millions of tourist to Denmark.
